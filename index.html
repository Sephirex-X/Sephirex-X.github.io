I am currently a final year Master student supervised by prof. Wankou Yang at the School of Automation, Southeast University, China. Expected to graduate in 2025. I obtained my B.E. degree in Automation (Transportation Information and Controls) from Chang'an University, China, in 2022 with Summa Cum Laude.

Selected Publications
Learning Multiple Probabilistic Decisions from Latent World Model in Autonomous Driving
Lingyu Xiao, Jiang-Jiang Liu, Sen Yang, Xiaofan Li, Xiaoqing Ye, Wankou Yang, Jingdong Wang.
Under review, 2024  
Paper / Page / Code

Exploring the feasibility of deriving decisions from an autoregressive world model through the formulation of multiple probabilistic hypotheses. Achieving expert-level performance on Waymax.
EasyChauffeur: A Baseline Advancing Simplicity and Efficiency on Waymax
Lingyu Xiao, Jiang-Jiang Liu, Xiaoqing Ye, Wankou Yang, Jingdong Wang.
Preprint, Arxiv , 2024
Paper / Supp. Videos

Suggesting the potential research shift from network architectures to training strategies, data efficiency, and robust evaluation methods in deep-learning based autonomous driving planner.
Domain Adaptive Depth Completion via Spatial-Error Consistency
Lingyu Xiao, Jinhui Wu, Junjie Hu, Ziyu Li, Wankou Yang.
Under review, Pattern Recognition (PR), 2024

Designed a novel training framework to solve the UDA problem in Depth Completion based on a domain/structural-agnostic property. Achieving SOTA on CARLA->KITTI and VKITTI->KITTI.
ADNet: Lane Shape Prediction via Anchor Decomposition
Lingyu Xiao, Xiang Li, Sen Yang, Wankou Yang.
International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV), 2023
Paper / Poster / Code

Addressing limitations on traditional anchor-based lane detection methods and develop a novel detection network accordingly. Achieving SOTA on VIL-100.
